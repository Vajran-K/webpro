Anna Auditorium

This iconic auditorium can seat up to 1,800 people and spans 15,436 sq. feet. It's decked out with centralized air conditioning, Wi-Fi, a stellar stage, and green rooms. Located next to the volleyball and basketball courts, it's a campus landmark.

Dr. M. Channa Reddy Auditorium

With a seating capacity of 469, this auditorium is perfect for mid-sized events and conferences.

Dr. Ambedkar Auditorium

Located in the Technology Tower, this 2,447 sq. ft. space seats 198 and is ideal for intimate gatherings and seminars.

Kamaraj Auditorium

Also in the Technology Tower, this auditorium offers seating for 128 in a 2,447 sq. ft. area, suitable for workshops and lectures.

Era Sezhiyan Auditorium

Situated in the Gandhi Block, this auditorium accommodates 462 attendees, making it versatile for various events.
